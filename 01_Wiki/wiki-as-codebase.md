# Wiki as Codebase

A powerful metaphor for the [[llm-wiki-pattern]] is treating the personal knowledge base as a software project.

## The Metaphor
*   **The Wiki is the Codebase:** A structured, interlinked collection of logic (knowledge).
*   **Obsidian is the IDE:** The environment where the codebase is viewed, searched, and debugged.
*   **The LLM is the Programmer:** The agent responsible for writing, refactoring, and maintaining the code.
*   **The Human is the Architect:** The one who sets the vision, curates the sources (requirements), and directs the exploration.

## Engineering Practices for Knowledge
*   **Refactoring:** Periodically "linting" the wiki to combine redundant notes or split over-large ones.
*   **Version Control:** Using **Git** to track changes, branch new research ideas, and collaborate with others.
*   **Vibe-Coding:** Using the LLM to quickly script custom tools (like search scripts or frontmatter fixers) to help manage the vault.
