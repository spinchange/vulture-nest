# Collective IQ

**Collective IQ** is a concept proposed by [[augmenting-human-intellect|Douglas Engelbart]] regarding the ability of a group or organization to effectively solve complex, "wicked" problems.

## The Scaling of Augmentation
Engelbart believed that by augmenting individuals with the **H-LAM/T system**, we could eventually augment the entire social collective. 
*   **Synergy:** The Collective IQ is not just the sum of individual intelligences but the result of how well they share, navigate, and build upon a common "Symbolic World."
*   **Collaborative Wiki:** In a team setting, the [[llm-wiki-pattern]] serves as the shared memory that maintains the Collective IQ, ensuring that insights from one member (or one project) are available to all.

## Modern Application
The use of LLMs to maintain team wikis (fed by Slack, transcripts, and docs) is a direct attempt to increase Collective IQ by reducing the "bookkeeping" friction of organizational knowledge.
