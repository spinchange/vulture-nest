# The Compounding Artifact

A core principle of the [[llm-wiki-pattern]] is that knowledge should be compiled once and then kept current, rather than re-derived on every query.

## Characteristics
*   **Incremental Synthesis**: Each new source updates the existing knowledge base.
*   **Pre-computed Connections**: Cross-references and contradictions are addressed at ingestion time.
*   **Growing Value**: The wiki becomes more useful as more sources and queries are processed, mirroring the concept of a [[memex]] trail.
