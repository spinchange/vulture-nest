# PKM Software Landscape

The modern PKM landscape is divided into different philosophies of information organization, often blending [[outliners|outlining]] and long-form writing.

## Modern Networked Tools
*   **Obsidian:** The "IDE" of PKM. Local-first, markdown-based, and highly extensible via plugins. It is the primary host for the [[llm-wiki-pattern]].
*   **Logseq:** A local-first, open-source outliner that supports both Markdown and [[org-mode]]. It bridges the gap between the block-level atomicity of [[roam-research]] and the data ownership of Obsidian.
*   **Tiantu / Reflect:** Newer entrants focusing on speed, minimalist aesthetics, and integrated AI features.

## The Hybrid Approach
Many practitioners use a **Logseq -> Obsidian** pipeline:
1.  **Logseq (Outliner):** For messy thinking, interstitial journaling, and rapid capture.
2.  **Obsidian (Long-form):** For refined synthesis, permanent storage, and building the [[the-compounding-artifact]].
