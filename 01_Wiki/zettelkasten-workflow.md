# Zettelkasten Workflow

The power of a [[zettelkasten]] comes from its disciplined workflow, which transforms consumption into active synthesis.

## 1. Capture
Write **Fleeting Notes** immediately when you encounter an interesting idea. Don't worry about formatting; just capture the thought.

## 2. Process
Convert your fleeting or literature notes into **Permanent Notes**. This requires thinking about how the new idea fits into your existing web of knowledge.

## 3. Link
Explicitly connect the new note to others. Ask:
*   "What does this contradict?"
*   "What does this support?"
*   "What trail of thought does this belong to?"

## 4. Retrieve
When working on a project, follow your "trails" of links. Structure emerges bottom-up as you navigate the connections you've previously established.

## Relationship to [[llm-wiki-pattern]]
The LLM automates the **Process** and **Link** stages by suggesting connections and summarizing sources, effectively turning raw text into literature and permanent notes.
