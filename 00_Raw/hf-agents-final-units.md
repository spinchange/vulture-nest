# Hugging Face Agents Course - Unit 0: Onboarding & Unit 4: Final Assignment

Source: [Hugging Face Agents Course](https://hf.co/learn/agents-course/)

## Unit 0: Foundations
* **Ollama:** A tool for running LLMs locally.
* **LiteLLM:** A provider-agnostic bridge allowing `smolagents` to communicate with local models (e.g., `LiteLLMModel`).
* **Community:** The Hugging Face Discord serves as the "Collective IQ" hub for the course.

## Unit 4: Evaluation (GAIA)
* **GAIA (General AI Assistants):** A benchmark of 466 real-world tasks that are simple for humans but challenging for AI.
* **Core Principles:** Multi-hop reasoning, multimodal understanding, and non-gameability.
* **Levels:**
    * Level 1: < 5 steps, minimal tools.
    * Level 2: 5-10 steps, complex coordination.
    * Level 3: Long-term planning, advanced tool integration.

## Advanced Protocols
* **MCP (Model Context Protocol):** Anthropic's standard for connecting LLMs to external data/tools (the "USB-C for AI").
* **A2A (Agent-to-Agent):** Google's protocol for interoperability between different autonomous agents.
